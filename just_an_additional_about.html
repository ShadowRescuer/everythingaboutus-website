How RescueFam101 Started

PERSEVERANCE
"As a rescuer, we, Adtuyon Emergency Rescue Unit (AERU), we
testament to the power of perseverance. In the face of adversity, we
stands strong, fueled by an unyielding determination to save lives. we
understand that every second counts, and we are committed to
pushing through, no matter how challenging the circumstances may
be. we are not deterred by the darkness, the storm, or the magnitude
of the disaster. Instead, we are inspired by the resilience of the human
spirit, the strength ofunity, and the hope that every new day brings.
we are the rescuer, and we will not rest until every life is safe. our
perseverance is our promise to humanity."
